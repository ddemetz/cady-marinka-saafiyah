How To Play

This is a choose your own adventure game, where each choice you make allows you to go further in the story, and consequently change your ending.

Instructions:
1. To play, you have to choose your story, and click on the "story" button
2. Then you'll be presented with a storyline and some backstory as well
3. After reading, you'll be given a few choices, click on the choice you'd like to pursue
4. Keep going!
